## Why creators are leaving ManyChat in 2026

![Why creators are leaving ManyChat in 2026: free plan cut 97.5%, per-contact billing, and a $29/mo AI add-on](/blog/why-leaving-manychat-2026.webp)

Creators are leaving ManyChat because of price, not features. In March 2026, ManyChat cut its free plan from 1,000 active contacts to 25, a 97.5% reduction, and now bills per active contact on every paid tier. AI replies, which used to feel core, now sit behind a separate $29/mo add-on.

Here is what that means in practice. An "active contact" is any person you interact with in a billing month. Paid plans start at $14/mo (Essential), climb to $29/mo (Pro) and $69/mo (Business, 7,500 contacts), and charge overages per contact above your cap. The structure is fine if your audience is small and static. It punishes you the moment automation works and your contact count climbs. Your bill grows precisely because you are succeeding. That is not a feature. It is a tax on growth.

The free-tier cut was the trigger, but per-contact pricing is the real reason people are shopping. A creator with 10,000 engaged followers can hit a monthly bill that rivals real business software, for what is fundamentally comment-to-DM and a few keyword flows. (ManyChat documented the change on [its own pricing-update blog post](https://manychat.com/blog/manychat-pricing-is-changing/) and the [Active Contacts definition is in its Help Center](https://help.manychat.com/hc/en-us/articles/14281409288604-Billing-FAQ).)

## What to look for in a ManyChat alternative

Not every "cheaper" tool is actually a good trade. Five things separate a real ManyChat replacement from a downgrade.

### Flat-rate vs per-contact pricing

This is the whole reason most people are here. Flat-rate means one fixed monthly fee no matter how many people you message. Per-contact means your bill rises with your audience. For Instagram automation, flat-rate is almost always cheaper at scale, and it makes your costs predictable. Five of the seven tools below are flat-rate.

### Official Instagram API

Any tool worth using runs on [Meta's official Instagram API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/). Tools that ask for your Instagram password and automate through a logged-in session are scraping, and scraping is how accounts get restricted or banned. A $2/mo tool that risks your account is the most expensive option on the list. Every tool here is official-API.

### Instagram depth vs multi-channel bloat

ManyChat spreads across Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, SMS, and Telegram. If you only automate Instagram, you are paying for surface area you never touch. A purpose-built Instagram tool usually does the IG-specific jobs (comment-to-DM, story-reply where supported, keyword triggers) better, for less.

### Built-in email capture

This is the feature most "cheap" tools quietly skip. If you run funnels (a coach booking calls, a D2C brand collecting leads), capturing the email inside the DM and syncing it to your list is the entire point. Among the cheapest tools, only some offer it natively.

### Real monthly price and migration effort

Look at the lowest plan that actually includes Instagram DM automation, not the headline number. Then check how hard it is to move: rebuilding three keyword flows takes an afternoon, not a quarter.
