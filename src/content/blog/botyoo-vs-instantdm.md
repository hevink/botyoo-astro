Botyoo and InstantDM are two of the cheapest flat-rate Instagram DM tools that run on the official API. The headline difference is price: Botyoo is $3.60/mo and InstantDM is $9.99/mo, about 2.8x more. But price isn't the whole story, so here's the honest comparison, including the places InstantDM is the better buy.

I build Botyoo, so I have a side. Every number below is public, and I've flagged each spot where InstantDM wins.

## The quick verdict

Botyoo wins on price and Instagram-only simplicity; InstantDM wins on breadth, story replies, scheduling, Facebook automation, and mobile apps. Both include email capture and AI. For a solo creator who wants the lowest flat price, Botyoo is the better fit. For someone who wants a wider toolkit, InstantDM earns its premium.

- **Pick Botyoo if** you want the lowest flat price for Instagram comment-to-DM, with email capture and AI included.
- **Pick InstantDM if** you want a broader toolkit (story replies, scheduling, Facebook and Meta-ads automation) or native mobile apps.
- **Price gap:** Botyoo $3.60/mo vs InstantDM $9.99/mo, both flat-rate on the official Instagram API.

## Features compared

The two tools are close on the Instagram essentials and diverge on breadth.

**Where they tie:** both run comment-to-DM automation and keyword-triggered auto-replies on the official Instagram API, both offer AI-assisted replies, and both give you an analytics view of what's converting. For a straightforward comment-to-DM funnel, either works.

**Where Botyoo leads:** price and focus. At $3.60/mo it is about a third of InstantDM's $9.99 for the same core comment-to-DM-plus-email-capture workflow, both include AI in the flat price, and Botyoo's single-channel focus means no broader toolkit to navigate or pay for if Instagram is all you need.

**Where InstantDM leads:** breadth. InstantDM includes story-reply automation (which Botyoo doesn't have yet), post scheduling, Facebook and Meta-ads automation, and native mobile apps for running flows from your phone. If you want more than Instagram comment-to-DM, that wider toolkit is real value Botyoo doesn't match today.

## Account safety and the official API

Both Botyoo and InstantDM are safe because both run on [Meta's official Instagram API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/), not password-based scraping. Botyoo is Meta-approved and InstantDM is a Meta Business Partner. Neither asks for your Instagram password or automates through a logged-in browser session, which is the pattern that gets accounts restricted. On safety this is a tie, so the decision comes down to price and breadth, not risk.

## FAQ

### Is Botyoo cheaper than InstantDM?

Yes. Botyoo is $3.60/mo versus InstantDM's $9.99/mo Pro plan, about a third of the price, or roughly $43 a year versus $120. Both are flat-rate on the official Instagram API, so neither charges per contact, and both include AI replies and native email capture, so the main difference is simply that Botyoo costs about a third as much.

### What does InstantDM do that Botyoo doesn't?

InstantDM offers story-reply automation, post scheduling, Facebook and Meta-ads automation, and native mobile apps. Botyoo is Instagram-focused and does not yet offer story replies or scheduling, so InstantDM is the broader tool if you need those, while Botyoo is the cheaper, more focused one.

### Does Botyoo or InstantDM have email capture?

Both have built-in email capture inside DM flows. InstantDM's $9.99/mo Legend Pro plan collects email (and phone) in the DM, and Botyoo includes it at $3.60/mo. On this feature the tools are level; the difference is price.

### Is InstantDM's AI better than Botyoo's?

Both offer AI-assisted DM replies on the official API, included in their flat plans with no credit metering, so neither charges extra in a busy month. Neither is dramatically more capable for everyday comment-to-DM; the practical difference is price, Botyoo's AI is included at $3.60/mo versus InstantDM's $9.99.

### Are Botyoo and InstantDM safe to use?

Yes, both are safe. Botyoo is Meta-approved and InstantDM is a Meta Business Partner, and both run on the official Instagram API rather than password-based scraping. Neither puts your account at the kind of risk that unofficial, login-based tools do.

### Can I use both Botyoo and InstantDM on the same account?

You can connect an Instagram account to either tool, but running two automation tools on the same account at once is not recommended, because overlapping triggers can fire duplicate DMs. The clean approach is to rebuild your flows in one tool, test, then disconnect the other.

## The bottom line

Botyoo and InstantDM both run on the official Instagram API at a flat rate, so this isn't a safety or pricing-model decision, it's a feature-and-cost one. Botyoo wins for solo creators who want the lowest price ($3.60 vs $9.99) for comment-to-DM with email capture and AI included. InstantDM wins for people who want more than that, story replies, scheduling, Facebook automation, and mobile apps. Both are flat-rate, official-API, and capture emails; pick on price and focus versus breadth.
