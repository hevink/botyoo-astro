Botyoo and LinkDM both run Instagram comment-to-DM on the official API, but they're built for different people: Botyoo is $3.60/mo for a single creator, LinkDM is $19/mo and bundles three accounts. So the comparison isn't really "which is cheaper", it's "how many accounts are you running". Here's the honest breakdown, including where LinkDM is the better buy.

I build Botyoo, so I have a side. Every number below is public, and I've flagged where LinkDM wins.

## The quick verdict

Botyoo wins for single creators on price and email capture; LinkDM wins for small agencies that need multiple accounts and a long track record. For one Instagram account, Botyoo is about a fifth of the price. For three accounts, LinkDM's bundle is competitive on the per-account math.

- **Pick Botyoo if** you run one account and want the lowest flat price plus native email capture.
- **Pick LinkDM if** you manage up to three accounts or want an established, agency-friendly tool.
- **Price gap:** Botyoo $3.60/mo (single) vs LinkDM $19/mo (3 accounts), both official-API.

## FAQ

### Is Botyoo cheaper than LinkDM?

For a single account, yes, by a lot: Botyoo is $3.60/mo versus LinkDM's $19/mo, about a fifth of the price, or roughly $43 a year versus $228. For three accounts the gap narrows, since LinkDM's $19 covers all three at about $6.33 each. Botyoo wins on single-account price; LinkDM is competitive for multi-account use.

### Is LinkDM worth $19 a month?

LinkDM is worth $19/mo if you genuinely run two or three accounts, because the bundle works out to about $6.33 per account with a 25,000-DM allowance and a track record since 2019. For a single creator, it's overkill, and Botyoo delivers the core comment-to-DM and email-capture workflow for $3.60/mo.

### Does LinkDM have email capture?

LinkDM's email capture is partial compared with Botyoo's native, built-in email capture inside DM flows. If collecting emails mid-conversation is central to your funnel, Botyoo handles it directly at $3.60/mo; with LinkDM you may need a separate tool for full email-capture workflows.

### Is LinkDM safe to use?

Yes. LinkDM is a Meta Business Partner running on the official Instagram API, like Botyoo, so it doesn't risk your account the way password-based scraping tools do. Both are safe; the decision is about price, account count, and email capture.

### Which is better for an agency, Botyoo or LinkDM?

For an agency managing multiple client accounts, LinkDM is the stronger fit: three accounts on one plan, a high DM allowance, and years of track record. Botyoo is built around single-account focus and the lowest price, so it suits solo creators better than agencies juggling several profiles.

### Can I manage multiple accounts on Botyoo instead of LinkDM?

Botyoo is built around single-account focus and the lowest price, so for genuinely managing two or three accounts, LinkDM's bundled $19 plan is the more natural fit. If you run one account and just want the cheapest, most capable single-account tool, Botyoo at $3.60/mo is the better value.

### Why is LinkDM more expensive than Botyoo?

LinkDM costs more because it bundles three accounts, a 25,000-DM allowance, and years of platform maturity into its $19/mo Pro plan, features aimed at small agencies. Botyoo is priced for a single creator at $3.60/mo, so for one account it's about a fifth of the cost; the gap reflects multi-account scope, not a better core workflow.

## The bottom line

This one comes down to account count. For a single Instagram account, Botyoo is the clear value at $3.60/mo, about a fifth of LinkDM's price, with native email capture and AI included. For a small agency running two or three accounts, LinkDM's $19 bundle and 2019-era track record make it the practical choice. Both are safe, official-API tools; pick Botyoo if you're solo, LinkDM if you're managing a roster.
