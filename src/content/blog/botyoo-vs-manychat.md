## Pricing: flat-rate vs per-contact

The biggest difference between Botyoo and ManyChat is the pricing model: Botyoo is flat-rate, ManyChat is per active contact plus a $29/mo AI add-on. That one design choice decides most of the cost comparison.

ManyChat's 2026 structure, [from its pricing page](https://manychat.com/pricing), has four tiers: Free (25 active contacts, cut from 1,000 in March 2026), Essential ($14/mo), Pro ($29/mo), and Business ($69/mo, 7,500 contacts), with per-contact overages above each cap. An "active contact" is anyone you interact with in a billing month, so the bill grows as your automation reaches more people. AI replies are not included on any tier; they cost an extra $29/mo.

Botyoo charges one flat $3.60/mo whether you message 50 people or 50,000, and AI replies are included. A creator who wants AI-assisted comment-to-DM pays $43/mo on ManyChat ($14 + $29 AI) before any overage. The same workflow on Botyoo is $3.60. That is the comparison in one line, and it is [why I built Botyoo](https://botyoo.com/about/). See [Botyoo's flat-rate plans](https://botyoo.com/pricing/) for the full breakdown.

## Features compared

On the core Instagram jobs, the two are closer than the price suggests. ManyChat pulls ahead on breadth; Botyoo matches it on the Instagram essentials and adds email capture cheaply.

**Where they tie:** both do comment-to-DM automation, keyword-triggered auto-replies, and analytics, both on the official Instagram API. For a creator running a comment-to-DM funnel, the day-to-day experience is similar.

**Where Botyoo leads:** email capture is built into Botyoo's DM flows at $3.60/mo, so you collect a lead mid-conversation and sync it to your list without another tool. AI replies are included rather than a $29/mo add-on. And because Botyoo is Instagram-only, the setup has less surface area to navigate.

**Where ManyChat leads:** ManyChat supports Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and SMS; Botyoo is Instagram-only by design. ManyChat also has story-reply automation, a shared team inbox, broadcast messaging, and a larger template and integration library. Botyoo does not yet offer story replies, a shared inbox, or broadcast. If you need those, ManyChat is the more complete tool today.

## Account safety and the official API

Both Botyoo and ManyChat are safe to use because both run on [Meta's official Instagram API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/) rather than scraping. Botyoo is Meta-approved; ManyChat is a Meta Business Partner. Neither asks for your Instagram password, and neither automates through a logged-in browser session, which is the unsafe pattern that gets accounts restricted. On safety, this comparison is a tie. The decision comes down to price, channels, and features, not risk.

## Where ManyChat still wins

ManyChat is the better choice if Instagram is only one of several channels you automate. Its multi-channel support across WhatsApp, Messenger, and SMS is the strongest in this category, and running everything in one tool can justify the per-contact premium. Its template ecosystem, integrations, and years of maturity also outclass any single-channel tool, Botyoo included. If you are a larger operation already invested in ManyChat flows across channels, switching to save on Instagram alone may not be worth the rebuild.

Botyoo is the better choice for Instagram-only creators who want the lowest predictable price.
