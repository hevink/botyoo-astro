Botyoo and ReplyRush both run Instagram comment-to-DM on the official API, but they price it in opposite ways: Botyoo is a flat $3.60/mo, ReplyRush is free up to 1,500 DMs a month and then charges per message. That single difference decides which one is cheaper for you, so here's the honest comparison, including where ReplyRush is the smarter start.

I build Botyoo, so I have a side. Every number below is public, and I've flagged where ReplyRush wins.

## The quick verdict

ReplyRush wins as a free on-ramp and if you also automate Facebook; Botyoo wins on predictable flat-rate pricing, native email capture, and cost at any real volume. For someone just starting who barely sends DMs, ReplyRush's free 1,500/mo is the better entry. For anyone running active comment-to-DM funnels, Botyoo's $3.60 flat is cheaper and easier to budget.

- **Pick Botyoo if** you want flat-rate pricing, native email capture, and AI replies included.
- **Pick ReplyRush if** you want a free tier to start, or you automate Facebook alongside Instagram.
- **Price gap:** Botyoo $3.60/mo flat vs ReplyRush free-then-per-DM (~$10/mo paid), both official-API.

## Pricing: flat-rate vs per-DM

The core difference is the pricing model. Botyoo charges $3.60/mo no matter how many DMs you send. [ReplyRush](https://www.replyrush.com) gives you 1,500 free DMs a month, then charges by message volume from around $10/mo. At very low volume ReplyRush is cheaper, it's free. The moment you send real volume, per-DM pricing gets unpredictable: a single comment-to-DM post that takes off can burn your allowance and bump you to the next tier mid-month, while [Botyoo's flat plan](https://botyoo.com/pricing/) doesn't move. ReplyRush is genuinely a good way to start; it's less suited to scaling.

## What you'll actually pay in a year

If you stay under 1,500 DMs a month, ReplyRush costs $0 and Botyoo costs about $43 a year, so ReplyRush wins outright at low volume. Once you're past the free cap and on per-DM pricing, costs climb with every campaign, and a busy creator can spend well over $120 a year. Botyoo stays at about $43 regardless of how many DMs you send.

Both tools are safe because both run on [Meta's official Instagram API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/) rather than scraping. Neither asks for your password, so safety is a tie, and the decision stays on pricing model, email capture, and channels. Botyoo is Meta-approved; ReplyRush is on the official API too.

## A real-world example

Say you run a weekly comment-to-DM post that delivers a free guide. In a quiet month you might send 800 DMs: ReplyRush is free, Botyoo is $3.60. Then one reel takes off and you send 6,000 DMs in a week. On ReplyRush you've blown through the free 1,500 and you're paying per message into a higher tier; on Botyoo you're still paying $3.60. That asymmetry, cheap when nothing's happening and expensive exactly when something is, is the core trade-off. If your volume is steady and low, ReplyRush's free tier is the bargain. If your best months are your busiest, flat-rate protects you from being charged for a win.

## FAQ

### Is Botyoo cheaper than ReplyRush?

It depends on volume. Under 1,500 DMs a month, ReplyRush is free and Botyoo is $3.60/mo. Once you exceed the free cap, ReplyRush's per-DM pricing climbs with volume and can pass $120 a year, while Botyoo stays flat at about $43 a year. For active senders, Botyoo is cheaper and far more predictable.

### What's the difference between flat-rate and per-DM pricing?

Flat-rate (Botyoo) is one fixed monthly fee regardless of how many DMs you send. Per-DM (ReplyRush) charges by message volume after a free allowance, so a viral post raises your bill. Flat-rate is easier to budget at scale; per-DM is cheaper only at low volume.

### Does ReplyRush have email capture?

ReplyRush's email capture is limited compared with Botyoo's native, built-in email capture inside DM flows. If collecting emails mid-conversation and syncing them to your list is central to your funnel, Botyoo handles it directly at $3.60/mo, while ReplyRush often means adding a separate tool.

### Is ReplyRush safe to use?

Yes. ReplyRush runs on Meta's official Instagram API, like Botyoo, so it doesn't risk your account the way password-based scraping tools do. Both are safe; the decision between them is about pricing model and features, not account safety.

### Can Botyoo automate Facebook like ReplyRush?

No. Botyoo is Instagram-only by design. ReplyRush automates Facebook Messenger alongside Instagram, so if Facebook is part of your funnel, ReplyRush covers a channel Botyoo doesn't. For Instagram-only automation, Botyoo's flat $3.60/mo and native email capture are the stronger fit.

### Can I start free on ReplyRush and move to Botyoo later?

Yes, and that's a sensible path. Use ReplyRush's free 1,500 DMs a month to validate that comment-to-DM works for your audience, then move to Botyoo's flat $3.60/mo once you're sending real volume and want a predictable bill with native email capture. Rebuilding a handful of flows takes an afternoon.

### Is ReplyRush good for Instagram comment-to-DM?

ReplyRush is a solid official-API comment-to-DM tool, especially at low volume thanks to its free 1,500-DM tier, and it covers Facebook as well as Instagram. Its weak spots versus flat-rate tools are cost predictability at scale and thinner native email capture, which is why higher-volume, list-building creators often move to Botyoo.

## The bottom line

Botyoo and ReplyRush both run safely on the official API, so this is a pricing-model decision. ReplyRush is the best free way to start and the pick if you automate Facebook too. Botyoo is the better long-term home for anyone running real comment-to-DM volume: flat $3.60/mo instead of an unpredictable per-DM bill, with native email capture and AI included. Start free on ReplyRush if you're testing; move to Botyoo when you want a predictable bill and lead capture built in.
