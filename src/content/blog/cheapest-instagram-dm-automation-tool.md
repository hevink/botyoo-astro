## What is the cheapest Instagram DM automation tool in 2026?

The cheapest Instagram DM automation tool in 2026 is Botyoo, at $3.60/mo on annual billing. It runs on the official Instagram API, does comment-to-DM and keyword auto-replies, and captures emails inside DM flows. The next-cheapest serious tool, InstantDM, costs $9.99/mo, nearly three times more. No other official-API tool comes in under $5/mo.

## Why most "cheapest" claims are wrong

"Cheapest" needs an asterisk, because three pricing tricks hide the real cost. Per-contact pricing (ManyChat) starts at $14/mo but climbs with every active contact, so your bill grows as your automation works. Per-DM pricing (ReplyRush) looks cheap until one viral comment-to-DM campaign blows through your cap. And the "$2/mo" tools you'll see advertised usually aren't on the official API at all — they automate through a logged-in session, which is scraping, and scraping is how accounts get restricted or banned. A tool that costs you your account isn't cheap; it's the most expensive mistake on this list. Every tool ranked below uses the [official Instagram API](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/instagram-platform/).

## How we ranked these tools

The ranking is by real monthly price, not the advertised headline. For flat-rate tools that's straightforward: the lowest plan that includes Instagram comment-to-DM. For per-contact (ManyChat) and per-DM (ReplyRush) tools, we used a representative monthly cost for a creator running active comment-to-DM funnels, because those models cost far more at scale than their entry price suggests. We only included tools on the official Instagram API; password-based scraping tools were disqualified on safety regardless of price. All eight prices were verified against public pricing pages in June 2026, and this category re-prices often, so treat the figures as current-as-of-now.

## Flat-rate vs per-contact: the hidden cost of "cheap" ManyChat

Flat-rate pricing charges one fixed fee no matter how many people you message; per-contact pricing charges more as your audience grows. For Instagram automation, flat-rate is almost always cheaper at scale. A flat $3.60/mo tool beats a per-contact tool the moment your contact list passes a few thousand. Here's the shape of it:

| Active contacts | Botyoo (flat) | ManyChat (per-contact) |
|---|---|---|
| 500 | $3.60/mo | ~$14/mo |
| 2,500 | $3.60/mo | ~$29/mo |
| 10,000 | $3.60/mo | ~$69/mo+ |

The flat line never moves. The per-contact line climbs exactly as your automation succeeds, which is why an advertised "$14/mo" tool can quietly become your most expensive subscription.

## What makes a cheap Instagram DM tool actually safe?

A cheap tool is only safe if it uses the official Instagram (Meta Graph) API rather than scraping or password login. Every tool in this ranking is official-API: Botyoo, InstantDM, ManyChat, CreatorFlow, and LinkDM are Meta-approved partners. The honest version of "cheapest" is Botyoo at $3.60/mo, the lowest price among tools that won't put your account at risk. Anything cheaper than that almost always means scraping, and a banned account is not a saving. Avoid any tool that asks for your Instagram password; the safe setup connects an [Instagram professional account](https://help.instagram.com/) through Meta's official authorization.
