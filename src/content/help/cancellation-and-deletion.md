# Cancellation and Account Deletion

You can cancel your subscription anytime. Account deletion is handled by request to keep your data protected.

## Cancelling your subscription

1. Open **Settings** → **Plan & Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm

You keep full access until the end of your current billing period. After that, automations pause and the workspace becomes read-only.

## Will I be charged again after cancelling?

No. Cancellation stops the next renewal. Whatever you've already paid covers you until the end of the current cycle.

## What happens to my data after cancellation?

Your data is retained for a reasonable period in case you reactivate. If you don't reactivate within that period, data is deleted per the [Privacy Policy](/privacy).

To export your data before cancelling, download your **Contacts CSV** from the Contacts page and screenshot any automation configurations you want to keep.

## Refunds

Botyoo offers a 7-day refund window from the initial purchase. Eligible refunds require a verifiable technical issue blocking core features.

Refunds are **not** issued for:

- Missed cancellations
- Partial use of a billing period
- Instagram-side bans or platform changes
- Terms of Service violations

To request a refund, email [hevin@botyoo.com](mailto:hevin@botyoo.com) with:

- Your account email
- The transaction ID
- A short description of the issue

Requests are evaluated within 5–7 business days. Eligible refunds reach your card within 7–10 business days.

## Deleting your account

There's no self-service delete button. Email [hevin@botyoo.com](mailto:hevin@botyoo.com) from the address on your account requesting deletion. The team confirms within 1–2 business days and removes your data per the Privacy Policy.

## What gets deleted

- All contacts, conversations, automations, campaigns, analytics
- Your Instagram access token (which revokes Botyoo's permissions in Meta)
- User profile and authentication records

## Deleting just one workspace

The workspace owner can delete a single workspace from the workspace switcher without affecting other workspaces under the same account.

## After deletion

You can re-create an account at any time with the same email. None of the deleted data can be recovered.
