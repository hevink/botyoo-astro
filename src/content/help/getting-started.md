# Getting Started with Botyoo

Welcome to Botyoo! This guide will walk you through connecting your Instagram account and launching your first automation.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Sign up at [app.botyoo.com](https://app.botyoo.com) using your email address. You'll be taken straight to your dashboard after verification.

## Step 2: Connect Instagram

1. From the dashboard, click **Connect Instagram**
2. You'll be redirected to Facebook's authorization page
3. Grant the required permissions — Botyoo needs access to manage your messages and comments
4. Once authorized, your Instagram account will appear in the dashboard

> **Note:** You need a **professional Instagram account** (Business or Creator) linked to a Facebook Page to use Botyoo.

## Step 3: Create Your First Automation

1. Go to **Automations** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Automation**
3. Choose a trigger (e.g., "Comment contains a keyword")
4. Add an action (e.g., "Send DM")
5. Toggle the automation **Active**

That's it — your first automation is live.
