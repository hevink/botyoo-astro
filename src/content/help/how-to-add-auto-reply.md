# How to add Auto Reply with variations

Auto Reply posts a public comment under the commenter's comment. Multiple variations stop your public replies from looking identical.

## Step 1: Open the automation

Open the comment-to-DM automation you want to add Auto Reply to.

## Step 2: Find Options → Auto Reply

Scroll to **Options** and turn **Auto Reply** on.

![Auto Reply toggle in Options](/help-screenshots/auto-reply-toggle.png)

## Step 3: Add reply variations

Write 3–5 short variations. Each one gets used in rotation so no two commenters see the same public reply.

![Auto Reply variations field with multiple entries](/help-screenshots/auto-reply-variations.png)

Examples:

- "Sent! Check your DMs 📩"
- "On the way ✨"
- "Check your DMs 👀"
- "DM'd you the link 🔗"

## Step 4: Save and activate

Each new comment triggers a public reply from your variations list, in addition to the DM.

## How rotation works

Botyoo cycles through your variations in order. If you have 4 variations and 12 comments, each variation is used 3 times across the batch.
