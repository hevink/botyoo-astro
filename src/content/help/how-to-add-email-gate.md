# How to add Email Gate

Email Gate asks for an email in the DM. The user replies, Botyoo saves it, and then sends the link.

## Step 1: Open the automation

Open the Instant Automation you want to add Email Gate to.

## Step 2: Find Options

Scroll to the **Options** section.

![Options section showing Email Gate](/help-screenshots/automation-options-email-gate.png)

## Step 3: Toggle Email Gate

Turn **Email Gate** on.

![Email Gate toggle on](/help-screenshots/email-gate-toggle-on.png)

## Step 4: Customize the ask message

Edit the "what's your email?" prompt. Defaults work, but make it sound like you.

![Email Gate message customization field](/help-screenshots/email-gate-message-edit.png)

## Step 5: Save and activate

The gate now runs before the DM is delivered.

## Where captured emails appear

Every email captured by Email Gate appears in the **Contacts** page, linked to that contact.

![Contacts page showing captured emails](/help-screenshots/contacts-with-emails.png)

You can:

- Filter by automation source
- Export to CSV from the Contacts page
- Push them into a campaign audience

![Export Emails button on Contacts page](/help-screenshots/contacts-export-button.png)
