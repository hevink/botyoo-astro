# How to add follow-up messages

Follow-ups send extra DMs on a delay, typically a reminder at 1 day and a "last chance" at 3 days.

## Step 1: Open the automation

Scroll to the **Follow-ups** section at the bottom of an Instant Automation.

![Follow-ups section in automation editor](/help-screenshots/followups-section.png)

## Step 2: Click "Add follow-up"

![Add follow-up button](/help-screenshots/followups-add-button.png)

## Step 3: Set the delay

Pick a number and a unit (minutes, hours, or days). Botyoo waits exactly that long after the first DM before sending.

![Follow-up delay field with number and unit](/help-screenshots/followup-delay-input.png)

## Step 4: Write the follow-up message

Compose the message. Attach an image or link if you want.

![Follow-up message editor](/help-screenshots/followup-message-editor.png)

## Step 5: Stack more follow-ups

Click **+ Add** again to stack additional steps. Each new step is added below the previous one with its own delay and message, Botyoo sends them in order, waiting the delay between each.

A common 3-step sequence:

| Step | Delay | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 day | Gentle reminder |
| 2 | 3 days | Last-chance |
| 3 | 7 days | Re-engagement |

## Step 6: Save

Save the automation. Follow-ups start running on every new contact captured.
