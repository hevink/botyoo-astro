# How to automate Story mentions

Story mentions are one of the most engagement-rich triggers. When someone tags you in their Story, you can auto-like, tag the contact, and send a personalized thank-you DM, without lifting a finger.

## Step 1: Click Create Automation

![Create Automation button in top bar](/help-screenshots/topbar-create-automation.png)

## Step 2: Pick the Story Mention template

In the template picker, choose **Reply To Story Mentions** under the Engage Your Audience category.

![Story Mention template card](/help-screenshots/template-story-mention.png)

## Step 3: Customize the DM

The template ships with a thank-you DM. Edit the text to sound like you, add an emoji or two, and reference the mention.

![Story mention DM editor](/help-screenshots/story-mention-dm-editor.png)

Example: `Thanks for the love, {{contact.name}}! 🙏 Re-sharing right now ❤️`

## Step 4: Add tagging (optional)

Add a **Tag contact** step or **Add to folder** step to mark them as a Supporter, useful for VIP segmentation later.

![Add to folder step with Supporters folder](/help-screenshots/story-mention-add-folder.png)

## Step 5: Activate

Toggle **Active**. Botyoo starts watching for Story mentions on the connected account.

## How it fires

| Event | Botyoo response |
|---|---|
| Someone tags you with `@yourhandle` in their Story | Trigger fires |
| Story is shared to feed | Like the mention (if step included) |
| Botyoo processes the contact | Tag / add to folder (if steps included) |
| Botyoo sends the DM | Personalized thank-you |
