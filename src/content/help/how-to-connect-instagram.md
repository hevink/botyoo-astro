# How to connect Instagram

Botyoo needs Instagram permissions to read comments and send DMs. The connection happens entirely through Meta's official OAuth, Botyoo never sees your password.

## Before you start

- Your Instagram must be a **Business** or **Creator** account
- It must be connected to a **Facebook Page**
- You must be the admin on that Page

> Use a desktop browser. Mobile Safari occasionally drops the OAuth redirect.

## Step 1: Open the Integration page

In the Botyoo sidebar, click **Integration**.

![Integration page in sidebar nav](/help-screenshots/sidebar-integration.png)

## Step 2: Click Connect Instagram

![Connect Instagram button on Integration page](/help-screenshots/integration-connect-button.png)

## Step 3: Meta authorization

You're redirected to Meta. On the page-picker screen, select the **Facebook Page** connected to the Instagram account you want to automate, then confirm the Instagram account underneath it. Click **Continue**.

If you have multiple Pages, pick the one linked to the correct IG handle, Botyoo will only read DMs and comments on the account you select here.

## Step 4: Grant every permission

Meta lists the permissions Botyoo needs. **Grant all of them**, declining one breaks the flow.

![Meta permission grant screen](/help-screenshots/meta-auth-permissions.png)

## Step 5: Back to Botyoo

You're returned to the Integration page. Your account appears with its avatar and handle.

![Integration page showing connected account](/help-screenshots/integration-connected.png)
