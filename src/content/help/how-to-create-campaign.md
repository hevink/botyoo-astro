# How to create a campaign

Campaigns are one-shot bulk DMs. Use them for flash sales, restock alerts, webinar reminders, and other time-sensitive messages.

## Step 1: Open Campaigns

Click **Campaigns** in the sidebar.

![Campaigns page in sidebar nav](/help-screenshots/sidebar-campaigns.png)

## Step 2: Click "+ New campaign"

![New campaign button](/help-screenshots/campaigns-new-button.png)

## Step 3: Name the campaign

Give it a clear name (e.g. "Summer Sale Announcement"). This is for internal tracking.

![Campaign name input](/help-screenshots/campaign-name-input.png)

## Step 4: Write the DM

In the **Message** field, write your message. Up to 1,000 characters. Use variable chips (`{{name}}`, `{{username}}`, `{{first_name}}`) to personalize.

![Campaign message editor with variable chips](/help-screenshots/campaign-message-editor.png)

## Step 5: Add an image or link (optional)

Drag an image into the upload zone. Click **Add Link** to insert a clickable URL.

![Campaign image upload and link section](/help-screenshots/campaign-image-link.png)

## Step 6: Pick the audience

Under **Send campaign to**, pick **By tag**, **By automation**, or both.

![Campaign audience picker, By tag and By automation](/help-screenshots/campaign-audience-picker.png)

## Step 7: Watch the live iPhone preview

The right pane shows an iPhone DM mockup that updates as you type. Use it to sanity-check the final message.

![Campaign iPhone preview](/help-screenshots/campaign-iphone-preview.png)

## Step 8: Click "Create campaign"

Botyoo queues the sends and starts delivering.

![Create campaign button at top-right](/help-screenshots/campaign-create-button.png)

## Step 9: Track delivery

The campaign's row in the list shows **Sent / Delivered / Failed** counts in real time.

![Campaign list with sent/delivered/failed metrics](/help-screenshots/campaign-list-metrics.png)

## Why some sends fail

The most common cause is Instagram's **24-hour messaging window**: if a contact hasn't DM'd you in the last 24 hours, the campaign send fails. Build the audience from contacts who recently interacted.
