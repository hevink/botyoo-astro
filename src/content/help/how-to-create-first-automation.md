# How to create your first automation

Your first automation is almost always a comment-to-DM: someone comments a keyword on your post, Botyoo replies with a personalized DM.

## Step 1: Click Create Automation

In the top bar of any page, click the blue **Create Automation** button.

![Create Automation button in top bar](/help-screenshots/topbar-create-automation.png)

## Step 2: Pick a template (or start from scratch)

The template picker opens. Choose **DM from Comments** (the simplest Instant) or **Start from Scratch** for a custom flow.

![Template picker with categories](/help-screenshots/template-picker.png)

## Step 3: Choose the post

Click the post selector and pick the Reel or post the automation should watch. Use **Browse more posts** to add more than one.

![Post selector with Instagram posts as thumbnails](/help-screenshots/automation-post-selector.png)

## Step 4: Set keywords

Enter the keywords that trigger the DM (e.g. `LINK`, `GUIDE`). Each chip is one keyword.

![Keyword input with chips for keywords](/help-screenshots/automation-keywords.png)

## Step 5: Write the DM

Compose the DM message. Add an image, links, and variables if you want.

![DM message editor with image upload and link section](/help-screenshots/automation-dm-editor.png)

## Step 6: Turn options on (optional)

In the Options section, enable **Welcome Message**, **Auto Reply**, **Follow Gate**, or **Email Gate** as needed.

![Options section with Follow Gate and Email Gate toggles](/help-screenshots/automation-options.png)

## Step 7: Activate

Toggle the automation **Active**. Botyoo starts watching the post immediately.

![Activate toggle at top of automation](/help-screenshots/automation-activate-toggle.png)

## Test it

From a second Instagram account, comment your keyword on the post. The DM should arrive within seconds.
