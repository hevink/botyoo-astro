# How to create a workspace

Each Botyoo workspace holds one Instagram connection. Create extra workspaces when you manage multiple accounts or brands.

## Step 1: Open the workspace switcher

Click the workspace pill at the top of the sidebar.

![Workspace switcher pill at top of sidebar](/help-screenshots/sidebar-workspace-pill.png)

## Step 2: Click "Create workspace"

The dropdown shows your existing workspaces and a **+ Create workspace** action. A counter (e.g. `1/10`) shows how many workspace slots you have left on your plan.

![Workspace dropdown with Create option and slot counter](/help-screenshots/workspace-dropdown-create.png)

## Step 3: Name the workspace

Enter a clear name like "Client A, Studio" or "My Personal IG". You'll see this everywhere in the UI.

![Create workspace modal with name field](/help-screenshots/workspace-create-modal.png)

## Step 4: Click Create

Botyoo creates the workspace and switches you into it. It starts empty, no automations, no contacts, no inbox history.

![Newly created empty workspace dashboard](/help-screenshots/workspace-new-dashboard.png)

## Step 5: Connect Instagram in the new workspace

Each workspace needs its own Instagram. Go to **Integration** in the sidebar, the page shows **Not Connected** with a **Connect Instagram** button, the same as the very first time you connected Botyoo. Click it and walk through Meta's authorization.

![Integration page with Connect Instagram button](/help-screenshots/integration-connect-button.png)

> See [How to connect Instagram](/help/how-to-connect-instagram) for the full Meta auth walkthrough.

## Plan limits

- Pro: 1 workspace
- Premium: 10 workspaces
- Enterprise: unlimited
