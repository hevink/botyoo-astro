# How to export contacts

Botyoo exports captured contacts as a CSV, useful for importing into your email tool, CRM, or any analytics dashboard.

## Step 1: Open the Contacts page

Click **Contacts** in the sidebar.

![Contacts page in sidebar nav](/help-screenshots/sidebar-contacts.png)

## Step 2: Filter (optional)

Use the **All Automations** filter to export only the contacts captured by one automation. Use the search bar to narrow further.

![Contacts page with automation filter open](/help-screenshots/contacts-filter-automation.png)

## Step 3: Click Export Emails

Top-right of the Contacts table.

![Export Emails button](/help-screenshots/contacts-export-button.png)

> **No emails yet?** If none of your contacts have a captured email, Botyoo shows a **"No contacts to export"** notice instead of downloading a file. Turn on **Email Gate** in one of your automations first, once contacts hand over an email, they'll show up here ready to export. See [How to add Email Gate](/help/how-to-add-email-gate).

## Step 4: Download the CSV

When at least one contact has an email, Botyoo generates a CSV and downloads it to your computer immediately.

The CSV includes:

- Username (or IGSID if username unavailable)
- Captured email
- Source automation
- Follower count
- First seen date
- Tags
- Folders

## Step 5: Import elsewhere

Upload the CSV to your destination, Mailchimp, ConvertKit, Klaviyo, a Google Sheet, your CRM. Match the columns to the destination's fields.

## Privacy

Only export and store emails you have permission to message. Most email tools require recipients to have opted in, Botyoo's Email Gate counts as opt-in for transactional follow-up but check your email provider's rules for bulk marketing.
