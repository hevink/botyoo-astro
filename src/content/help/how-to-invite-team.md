# How to invite team members

Workspace members see the same automations, contacts, inbox, and campaigns. Inviting one takes about ten seconds.

## Step 1: Open Workspace settings

Click **Settings** at the bottom of the sidebar, then the **Workspace** tab.

![Settings page with Workspace tab selected](/help-screenshots/settings-workspace-tab.png)

## Step 2: Enter their email

In the **Invite member** field, type the email address. Click **Invite**.

![Workspace settings showing Invite member input field](/help-screenshots/settings-invite-input.png)

## Step 3: They're added instantly

Botyoo adds the teammate to your workspace the moment you hit **Invite**, no email confirmation step. The next time they sign in (or refresh, if they're already signed in), the workspace appears in their workspace switcher.

> Make sure the email you enter exactly matches the one they use to sign in to Botyoo. If they don't have a Botyoo account yet, ask them to [sign up](/help/how-to-sign-up) first with the same email, then run the invite.

## Step 4: They show up in Members

They appear immediately in the Members list with the **Member** role.

![Workspace Members list with new member added](/help-screenshots/workspace-members-list.png)

## Roles

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| Owner | Everything including billing, IG connection, removing members |
| Member | Everything except billing, IG connection changes, removing members |

## Removing a member

Only the Owner can. From the Members list, click the row's remove action and confirm. Access is revoked immediately.

![Remove member confirmation dialog](/help-screenshots/workspace-remove-member-dialog.png)
