# How to manage billing and your plan

Everything billing-related lives in **Settings → Plan & Billing**.

## Step 1: Open Plan & Billing

Click **Settings** at the bottom of the sidebar, then the **Plan & Billing** tab.

![Settings with Plan & Billing tab](/help-screenshots/settings-billing-tab.png)

## Step 2: Review your current plan

The top card shows:

- Plan name (Pro / Premium / Enterprise)
- Status (Active / Cancelling / Past due)
- Next renewal date
- Billing cycle (Monthly / Quarterly / Yearly)
- Subscription ID

![Plan card with subscription details](/help-screenshots/billing-plan-card.png)

## Step 3: Upgrade or downgrade

Click **Upgrade** (or **Change plan**). Pick the new plan and billing cycle. Complete checkout.

![Plan selection screen](/help-screenshots/billing-upgrade-modal.png)

The new plan kicks in immediately. You keep all existing automations, contacts, and inbox history.

## Step 4: Switch to annual billing

Yearly billing reduces the effective monthly cost significantly, annual Pro works out to about $3.60/mo. Pick **Yearly** during upgrade for the discount.

![Billing cycle selector with Monthly / Quarterly / Yearly](/help-screenshots/billing-cycle-selector.png)

## Step 5: View invoices

Scroll to the **Payments** section. Every charge is listed with a downloadable invoice.

![Payments list with invoice downloads](/help-screenshots/billing-payments-list.png)

If the section says "No payments found" right after a successful payment, give it up to 24 hours to sync. Contact support if it stays empty.

## Step 6: Update payment method

When a card on file is about to expire, the Plan & Billing tab shows an **Update payment method** button. Click it, enter the new card details in the secure form, and save. The next renewal will use the updated card automatically.

## Cancel subscription

Cancellations are handled by the Botyoo support team, there's no self-serve cancel button in-app right now. Open the **💬 chat widget** in the bottom-right corner of any Botyoo page and ask to cancel. The support team confirms within one business day. You keep full access until the end of the current billing period.
