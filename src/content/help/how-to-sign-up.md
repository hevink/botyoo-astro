# How to sign up for Botyoo

Botyoo takes about 60 seconds to sign up. You can use Google OAuth or a magic link sent to your email.

## Step 1: Open the sign-up page

Go to [app.botyoo.com](https://app.botyoo.com) and click **Sign up**.

![Sign-up screen with email field and Continue with Google button](/help-screenshots/signup-page.png)

## Step 2: Choose your sign-in method

- **Google**, one click. Botyoo uses your Google email and avatar.
- **Email + magic link**, enter your email and Botyoo sends a one-time login link.
- **Email + password**, set a password if you prefer the classic flow.

![Sign-up method options](/help-screenshots/signup-methods.png)

## Step 3: Verify your email

If you used email instead of Google, click the verification link Botyoo sends. The link logs you in automatically.

![Verification email preview](/help-screenshots/signup-verification-email.png)

## Step 4: Land on the dashboard

Once verified, you're taken to your dashboard. You'll see DM Blasts and New Follower analytics panels, both empty until you set up your first automation.

![Empty dashboard after first sign-in](/help-screenshots/dashboard-empty.png)
