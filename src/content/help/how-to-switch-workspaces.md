# How to switch between workspaces

If you belong to more than one workspace, you can switch between them without logging out.

## Step 1: Open the workspace switcher

Click the workspace pill at the top of the sidebar.

![Workspace pill at top of sidebar showing current workspace](/help-screenshots/sidebar-workspace-pill.png)

## Step 2: Pick a workspace

The dropdown lists every workspace you belong to. A checkmark shows your current one. Click any other workspace to switch.

![Workspace dropdown listing multiple workspaces with check on current](/help-screenshots/workspace-dropdown-switching.png)

## Step 3: You're in

Botyoo reloads the dashboard with the new workspace's data, its automations, contacts, inbox, and integration.

![Dashboard after switching to a different workspace](/help-screenshots/dashboard-after-switch.png)
