# How to target a campaign audience

Targeting is the difference between a 5% campaign and a 30% campaign. Botyoo gives you two audience filters that can be combined.

## Step 1: Open a new or existing campaign

Open the campaign builder.

## Step 2: Find "Send campaign to"

Scroll to the **Send campaign to** section.

![Send campaign to section](/help-screenshots/campaign-send-to-section.png)

## Filter By tag

Pick a tag from the dropdown. Every contact with that tag becomes part of the audience.

![By tag dropdown with tags listed](/help-screenshots/campaign-by-tag-dropdown.png)

Good for:

- "Everyone tagged as `paid-customer`"
- "Everyone tagged as `interested-in-courses`"

## Filter By automation

Pick the automation that captured the contacts. Every contact from that automation becomes the audience.

![By automation dropdown with automations listed](/help-screenshots/campaign-by-automation-dropdown.png)

Good for:

- "Everyone who entered through the Summer Sale comment-to-DM"
- "Every commenter on the launch Reel"

## Combine both for the intersection

Set both filters. The audience becomes contacts who match **both**, narrower and higher-intent.

![Campaign with both filters set](/help-screenshots/campaign-both-filters.png)

Good for:

- "VIP-tagged contacts captured by the Course Funnel automation"

## Audience size preview

The campaign builder shows the audience size before you send. Use this to confirm you're targeting the right number: too few means you're missing leads, too many usually means your filter is too loose.

![Audience size preview](/help-screenshots/campaign-audience-size.png)
