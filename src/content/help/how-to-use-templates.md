# How to use automation templates

Botyoo ships 30+ templates across five categories, Instant, Engage, Get Leads, Convert & Sell, and Grow Your Audience.

## Step 1: Open the template picker

Click **Create Automation** in the top bar.

![Create Automation button in top bar](/help-screenshots/topbar-create-automation.png)

## Step 2: Browse categories

The picker groups templates by goal. Each card has a description and tags so you can scan quickly.

![Template picker with category sections, Instant, Engage, Get Leads, Convert & Sell, Grow](/help-screenshots/template-picker-categories.png)

## Step 3: Pick a template

Click any template card. Each card shows the template name, a one-line description, and category tags (e.g. `engagement`, `lead-gen`, `webinar`) so you know what it does at a glance. Clicking opens the automation pre-configured with example settings.

## Step 4: Customize

The template populates the fields. Edit:

- Post selection
- Keywords
- DM message text and image
- Follow Gate / Email Gate toggles

![Template loaded with example settings](/help-screenshots/template-loaded.png)

## Step 5: Activate

Toggle the automation **Active** to ship.

## Instant vs Flow templates

| Badge | What it means |
|---|---|
| ⚡ **Instant** | Single-trigger automation, form-based editor, includes built-in gates |
| 🔗 **Flow** | Multi-step visual flow, opens in the Flow Builder |
