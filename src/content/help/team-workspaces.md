# Team and Workspaces

A **workspace** is a self-contained Botyoo environment with its own Instagram connection, automations, contacts, inbox, and analytics. Use one workspace per Instagram account.

## Why workspaces

If you manage multiple Instagram accounts, say, three client brands or a personal account plus your business, each gets a separate workspace. Data, automations, and analytics stay isolated.

## How many workspaces can I have?

| Plan | Workspaces |
|---|---|
| Pro | 1 |
| Premium | Up to 10 |
| Enterprise | Unlimited |

## Creating a workspace

1. Click the workspace pill at the top of the sidebar
2. Choose **+ Create workspace**
3. Name it (e.g. "Client A, Studio")
4. Click **Create**

The new workspace starts empty. Connect Instagram in the Integration page to set it up.

## Switching workspaces

The workspace pill opens a dropdown listing every workspace you belong to. Click one to switch instantly.

## Inviting team members

1. Open **Settings** → **Workspace**
2. Enter the member's email in **Invite member**
3. Click **Invite**

The member receives a one-click invite link by email. If they're already logged into Botyoo, they join the workspace instantly with no extra steps.

## Roles

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Everything, including billing, Instagram connection, removing members |
| **Member** | Everything except billing, Instagram connection changes, removing members |

Granular per-permission roles aren't supported yet. Per-workspace permissions keep most teams safe.

## Removing a member

Only the Owner can. Settings → Workspace → find the member and click **Remove**. Access is revoked immediately.

## Moving automations between workspaces

Workspaces are isolated, so you can't move an automation directly. To replicate one in a different workspace, screenshot the configuration in the source workspace and rebuild it in the target.

## Deleting a workspace

The Owner can delete a workspace from the workspace switcher dropdown. Deletion is permanent, all contacts, conversations, and automations in that workspace are removed.
